About Us Page

Mardin is a non-profit organisation focused on serving Asian countried like India and Pakistan.
It is an online platform that aims to provide channels and resources to trusted non-governmental organisations across India
and help improve environment conservation, Health and hygiene, fight poverty and improving livelihood of homeless and people who live under the poverty line.
As a web portal, it helps raise funds and contributions from individuals in India and around the world
and then distributes these funds and touched the lies of thousands of people across the continent.
